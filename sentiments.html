The Perfect Way to Conduct a Virtual Demo and Ship Products Directly to Customers and Potential New Distributors!

Demo Drop Ship Order (DDSO), available only in the United States, Canada, Australia and New Zealand, allows you as the SeneGence Independent Distributor to process an order to be delivered at the home or office of your Demo Hostess and potentially their Guests (to be used in the event a Distributor does not have inventory at a Demo – SeneGence Distributors pay discounted wholesale!)  A great way to conduct your Demos VIRTUALLY!

This program enables every Distributor to sell the SeneGence product line at a Demo even if they do not have inventory on hand. DDSO is a “drop-ship” order system which means you can send products directly to your Hostess for them to distribute to their Demo Guests.

For more information and to place a Demo Drop Ship order, go to Back Office > Business > Customers > Demo Drop Ship Program. Available in USA, CAN, AUS and NZL only.
